Expert Vacuum Repair & Service

When your vacuum cleaner needs service or repair, we offer free diagnosis and estimate. We’ve built our reputation on providing good, honest service – if your vacuum can be repaired for a reasonable price, we’ll tell you. And if it isn’t worth fixing, we’ll let you know that, too. Whether you decide to repair your machine or not, the diagnosis is always no charge.

Many common vacuum repairs can be made for under $20. In addition, we often have used parts available that you can choose for us to use in your repair, which can help lower of the cost of fixing your vacuum.

Vacuum Tune-ups

Make sure your vacuum has maximum suction and is working at peak capacity. For less than $20, we’ll give your vacuum a tune-up, replace the bag and belt, and have it ready for pickup in just a day or two (unless a part needs to be special ordered).
