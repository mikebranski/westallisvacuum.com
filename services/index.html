Expert Vacuum Repair & Service

We’ve built our reputation on providing good, honest service – if your vacuum can be repaired for a reasonable price, we’ll tell you. And if it isn’t worth fixing, we’ll let you know that, too. Whether you decide to repair your machine or not, the estimate is always free.

Many common vacuum repairs can be made for under $20. In addition, we often have used parts available that you can choose for us to use in your repair, which can help lower of the cost of fixing your vacuum.
